## STRATEGY A: EXPAND EXISTING CHAPTERS

### Expansion Playbook (applies to every chapter)

Each existing chapter should be expanded with some combination of:

1. **"What Twenty Years Teaches" section** — Fill all 8 blank sections (~1,000 words each = 8,000 words total). Personal reflections, pattern recognition, war stories.

2. **Extended case studies** — Each chapter currently has 1-2 brief examples. Expand to 2-3 full case studies per chapter with real deal details, timeline, outcome, and lessons learned. Your 30+ company case study documents are the source.

3. **Annotated documentation excerpts** — Show actual covenant language (redacted/genericized) with line-by-line annotations. Your "Plain English Translations" series (15+ documents) is purpose-built for this.

4. **US vs. European comparison sections** — Most chapters discuss European markets. Add systematic comparisons using your "Loans vs. Bonds" series (20+ documents).

5. **Worked examples and calculations** — Where applicable, add step-by-step numerical examples (e.g., builder basket calculations, leverage ratio tests, excess cash flow waterfalls).

6. **Historical evolution sections** — How did this covenant/structure/practice evolve? Market data from 2005-2025.
