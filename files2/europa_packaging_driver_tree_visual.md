## Summary: The Driver Hierarchy

```
LEVEL 1: OPERATING DRIVERS (from FDD/CDD)
├── Volumes (by segment, geography, product)
├── Prices (ASP by segment, trends, pass-through)
├── Input costs (raw materials, energy, labour rates)
└── Efficiency metrics (yield, utilisation, productivity)
         │
         ▼
LEVEL 2: FINANCIAL STATEMENT DRIVERS
├── Revenue = Σ (Volume × ASP) by segment
├── COGS = Materials + Direct Labour + Variable Overhead
├── Opex = Fixed costs + Variable costs
└── EBITDA = Revenue - COGS - Opex
         │
         ▼
LEVEL 3: CASH FLOW DRIVERS
├── Working capital = f(days × activity levels)
├── Capex = Maintenance (fixed) + Growth (discretionary)
├── Interest = f(debt quantum × pricing)
└── Tax = f(EBT × rate, adjusted for timing)
         │
         ▼
LEVEL 4: CREDIT METRICS (what lenders care about)
├── Leverage = Debt / EBITDA
├── Coverage = EBITDA / Interest
├── Cash conversion = FCF / EBITDA
└── Liquidity = Cash + Undrawn RCF
```
