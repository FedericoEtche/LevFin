## 2. Recommended Actions

### A. New Chapter: Liability Management Exercises (LMEs)

**Source material:** 9 files from "LME and LME Blockers"
**Rationale:** LMEs have reshaped leveraged finance documentation since 2020. The 9fin research covers the full spectrum: uptier transactions (Serta, Chewy, J.Crew), blocker provisions, enabler provisions, cooperation agreements, and Fifth Circuit developments. No existing chapter addresses this. This is the single biggest gap in the book.

**Suggested chapter outline:**
- What is an LME and why it matters
- Uptier transaction mechanics (US and European approaches)
- Key case studies: Serta, J.Crew, Chewy
- LME blocker provisions (basic through next-level)
- LME enabler provisions
- Cooperation agreements between lenders
- Value leakage risks
- Fifth Circuit and evolving case law
- Documentation responses and market evolution

### B. New Chapter or Major Section: Covenant Suspension & Release Conditions

**Source material:** 2 files from "Covenant Suspension"
**Rationale:** Covenant suspension upon achieving investment grade is a critical but under-discussed mechanism. Could be a standalone chapter or a substantial section within `chapter_financial_covenants.txt`.
